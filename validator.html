frequently asked questions

What kind of support is available to students during the program, such as access to tutors or mentors?

We provide access to tutors and mentors for our students throughout their program. Our tutors are available through our online learning platform or email and respond within 24 hours. Our mentors are experienced professionals who offer career advice, skill development, and industry insights.

How long does it take to complete a program at the Validator School?

The length of a program at the Validator School varies depending on the program and the student's pace of study. We offer programs of different lengths, from short courses to full programs that take year to complete. The duration can be adjusted to fit the student's schedule and learning style.

What kind of career opportunities are available to graduates of the Validator School

The Validator School offers a range of programs that prepare graduates for various IT careers. Graduates may find employment in different industries and work for small businesses, startups, or large corporations. We provide career services, including job search assistance and networking opportunities, to help graduates find employment. Internships and co-op opportunities are also available to gain real-world experience.

Are there any opportunities for networking or connecting with other professionals in the field through the Validator School?

Yes, we provide various opportunities for networking and connecting with other IT professionals through the Validator Shool, including our online learning platform, social media groups, virtual events, webinars, career services. Our students can benefit from these resources to build their professional networks, gain industry insights, and prepare for their future careers.

What kind of certifications I will earn upon completing courses or programs at the online Validator School?

Upon completing a program at the Validator School, you will receive a certificate. The specific type of certificate will depend on the program you choose.

How are assessments and exams conducted in the Validator School, and what kind of support is available for test-taking?

At the Validator School, exams are taken online through our learning management system with online proctoring. We offer study materials, practice tests, and exam guides to support test-taking, and our instructors are available for help. If you need testing accommodations, please contact our student support team.
